Hey there! My name is Rachel Thomas and I'm a senior convergence journalism major at the University of Missouri. As an aspiring journalist I’m always looking for the next opportunity to collaborate and use multimedia to tell stories. That's why I've chosen the Convergence Journalism track with an emphasis in Multimedia Producing. The most impactful stories cannot be best understood with text alone. Great pieces are orchestrated with video, photos, interactive graphics and more to immerse your audience in your story.

​ My skills include: Video/audio storytelling and editing ​Proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro, Audition and After Effects DSLR photography and photo editing Proficient with the Nikon D7000 and Photoshop Infographic creation AP Style Interacting with dogs I am currently working as a multimedia journalists for the Columbia Missourian and Flatlands, a digital magazine owned by Kansas City Public Television. I am also working as a videography intern for the University of Missouri. I have experience working as a communications intern and convergence marketing intern, as well as recently producing content for the St. Louis Post-Dispatch. ​ In addition to journalism and storytelling, I'm passionate about ending sexual assault on my campus as an It's On Us committee member and survivor. ​ When I'm not researching or creating stories, you can find me making mini-movies with my iPhone, singing along while my neighbor plays the saxophone or eating my bodyweight in doughnuts at Harold's.
